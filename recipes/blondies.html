Blondies

Aah, blondies. The cakelike consistency of brownies, the velvety richness of vanilla, and the classic lovability of chocolate chips. These simple-to-make treats are perfect for family gatherings, game nights, or just to bring a little sweetness to your life.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter (melted)
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar (tightly packed)
  • 1 large egg (lightly beaten)
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/8 tsp baking soda
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup chocolate chips (or other desired mix-ins)

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F
  2. Lightly butter and flour an 8" by 8" pan.
  3. Whisk together the melted butter and sugar in a bowl. Add the egg and vanilla and whisk them in.
  4. Add the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt and whisk them all together.
  5. Add the chocolate chips and/or any other desired mix-ins. Up to 1 cup of extra ingredients can be added here.
  6. Spread the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 18 to 25 minutes. When a toothpick can be stuck in the center and come out clean, it's time for them to come out.
  7. Allow the blondies to cool on the counter.
  8. Cut into squares and serve.
